About

Environmental science with real-world relevance.

Shamina Israt Tithi is an Earth and Environmental Sciences educator and researcher based in Queens, New York. She holds a Master of Arts in Earth and Environmental Sciences from Brooklyn College, CUNY, and a Bachelor of Science in Forestry from the University of Chittagong.

Her academic and professional work connects environmental science, sustainability, GIS, soil research, forestry, climate resilience, environmental field inspection, and community engagement. As an Adjunct Lecturer at Brooklyn College, she teaches undergraduate students about dynamic Earth processes while designing lectures, assignments, interactive labs, and real-world learning activities.
